HEMA

A Brief Introduction

By: Nicholas Seemann

HEMA, or Historical European Martial Arts, are a modern attempt to bring back the martial arts techniques of European History through different methods such as analyzing texts that date as far back to the Roman Empire, to looking at old paintings depicting people fighting each other and attempting to link it to known fighting techniques. Another interesting fact is that European 'masters' of various martial arts were known to leave behind manuals that are coded in poetry, and so a good deal of effort is put into reading these poems and attempting to learn the actual techniques hidden within. This could range from hand-to-hand combat, the use of weapons, or grappling techniques. The most popular aspect which is focused on, however, is sword fighting.
